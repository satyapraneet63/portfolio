## 3D Printing & Hardware Mods

My 3D printing journey started with troubleshooting more than printing — and somehow that became the fun part.

I’ve modded everything from firmware to extruders. My current setup runs Klipper, controlled through a custom-built Raspberry Pi host, with sensor tuning, ADXL345 for input shaping, and an Orbiter V2 extruder. Yes, the cable management is always a work in progress.

I enjoy printing functional parts, miniatures, and calibration tools — though sometimes, printing *the printer* itself is half the hobby.
