## Art & Murals

Painting has always been my grounding habit. I’ve worked on large-scale murals, canvas pieces, and digital sketches — switching between traditional and digital media depending on my mood.

My style is a mix of realism and abstract, often blending geometric structure with flowing brushstrokes. I paint with a purpose, whether it's to relax, tell a story, or fill an empty wall with something meaningful.

If you're looking for a mural artist with an engineer’s precision and an artist’s instincts — let's talk.
